"First Intention : 초심 (初心)" is LK's 1st EP consisting of 4 songs.

2. Most Beautiful Produced by Scarecrow Beats & Lyrics by LK
3. Forgotten Love Produced by Peter Chang & Lyrics by LK, SeongJae Kim, and Yeeun Kim

Album cover designed by Nata

5. Dream Come True Produced by Scarecrow Beats & Lyrics by LK
